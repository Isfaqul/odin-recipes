Classic Maggie

I love Maggi and I love to experiment with various types and flavours. Today I have shared the classic vegetable masala maggi. Now when monsoon has arrived, I love to enjoy this hot veg masala maggi in the rains, followed by a hot cup of tea.

Ingredients

  • 2 packets Maggi
  • 4 cups water to boil Maggi
  • 1 cup onions
  • 1 cup tomatoes
  • ¼ cup red bell pepper
  • ¼ cup yellow bell pepper
  • ¼ cup green capsicum
  • 2 tablespoons carrot
  • 3 tablespoons oil
  • ¼ teaspoon turmeric powder
  • ½ teaspoon red chilli powder
  • ½ teaspoon coriander powder
  • ½ teaspoon cumin powder
  • ½ teaspoon salt

Steps In Making

  1. Place a pan over medium flame and add water in it. Once the water comes to a boil, add 2 packets of Maggi, without the Maggi masala and let it cook for 2-3 minutes or until the Maggi is 80% cooked.
  2. Now with the help of a sieve, drain the excess water and let the Maggi rest, till we prepare the rest of the things.
  3. To prepare the veggie masala, finely chop all the veggies, red and yellow bell peppers, onions, tomatoes and capsicum.
  4. Now place a pan over medium flame add oil in it. Once it turns hot, add the onions and cook till it turns translucent.
  5. Now add the carrots and cook for a minute.
  6. Further add the tomatoes and cook for another couple of minutes, till they turn soft.
  7. Now add the bell peppers and the green capsicum and mix well.
  8. Lastly add all the dried spices, red chilli powder, coriander powder, turmeric powder, salt, cumin powder and Maggi Masala and saute for couple of minutes.
  9. Finally add the boiled maggi and mix till everything is well combined.
  10. Serve hot!
