Creamy and Cheesy Italiano MAGGI Noodles Recipe

Ingredients :

  • 2 Packets

    MAGGI 2-Minute Noodles (Masala)

  • 1 Tablespoon

    Olive Oil

  • 1 Teaspoon

    Garlic (Grated)

  • 2 Tablespoon

    Basil Leaves (Chopped)

  • 0.5 Cup

    Green Capsicum (Chopped)

  • 0.25 Cup

    Red Capsicum (Chopped)

  • 0.25 Cup

    Yellow Capsicum (Chopped)

  • 2 Tablespoon

    Cheese Spread

  • 0.75 Cu

    Milk

Instruction :

  • Step 1

    Prepare ingredients as mentioned in the list.

  • Step 2

    Just heat the oil and fry the garlic for about a minute. Then, toss in the basil leaves and fry them for another minute. Add all the capsicums, saut√© for a couple of minutes and switch off the gas. Drop in the cheese spread and mix well.

  • Step 3

    Cook a packet of MAGGI Masala Noodles (As per the instructions on the pack) and when it's done, lovingly pour the cheese and capsicum over the MAGGI.

  • Step 4

    Serve hot!
